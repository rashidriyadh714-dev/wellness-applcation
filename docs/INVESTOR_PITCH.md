## Product Overview

### Core Features (MVP - Live Today)

#### 1. **Intelligent Dashboard**
- Real-time wellness scoring (0-100)
- 4 KPI cards (Wellness Score, BMI, Risk Level, Records)
- 7-day wellness trending with predictive forecasts
- Personalized health recommendations

#### 2. **AI Wellness Chatbot**
- Natural language health coaching
- Sleep, fitness, nutrition, stress guidance
- Context-aware personalized advice
- Available 24/7 via conversational interface

#### 3. **Predictive Analytics Engine**
- 90-day wellness forecasting
- Health risk assessment (obesity, cardiovascular, chronic disease)
- Pattern analysis (sleep quality, activity trends)
- Untapped opportunity identification

#### 4. **Automated Wellness Automation**
- Personalized daily reminders (adaptive frequency)
- AI-generated workout routines (age & fitness-adaptive)
- Smart health insights based on behavior
- Trend forecasting with week-by-week projections

#### 5. **Goal & Habit Tracking**
- Goal management with progress tracking
- Habit streak building & consistency rewards
- Milestone achievements & badge gamification
- Personal records & achievement tracking

#### 6. **Real-Time Alert System**
- Critical health alerts (abnormal metrics)
- Age-based health screening reminders
- Emergency protocol guidance
- Integration with emergency contacts

#### 7. **Wearable Integrations**
- Fitbit, Apple Health, Google Fit APIs
- Garmin, Whoop, Oura Ring compatibility (roadmap)
- Real-time data synchronization
- Multi-device health aggregation
